How Do I Get There?

  Road 1-A, Bulabog, Brgy. Balabag
Boracay Island, Malay, Aklan 5608
Philippines

MNL Boracay Beach Hostel is located at the heart of the world’s best island -- BORACAY. Right in the middle of white beach (for amazing sand, sunbathing and partying) and Bulabog beach (for kite surfing and adventures).

We are 2 minutes walk to Bulabog beach front, 5 minutes walk from the main road, 10 mins walk from white beach.

Just the right mix if you want some quiet retreat or an action packed night!

From Caticlan (MPH) Airport:

    ﻿

    Take a tricycle to Caticlan Jetty Port

    Take a boat to boracay island (Php 25 + Php 100 terminal fee + Php 75 environmental fee)

    ﻿

    Upon arrival on boracay island, take tricycle and have them bring you to MNL Boracay Beach Hostel (in front of Danaru Spa) -- Php 150

    ﻿

﻿From Kalibo (KLO) Airport​: ﻿

    Uppon arrival, take Southwest Airport to Hotel transfers to MNL Boracay Beach Hostel, in front of Danaru Spa (Php 500)

﻿ ﻿
