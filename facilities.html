About Us

Since 2013, MNL Hostel has been passionate about providing the best hostel experience in the Philippines.

It started when friends with a passion for travel converted an old house into one of Manila's first hostels. Branching out into the island paradise Boracay, the backpackers home was made to be a welcome hug for old friends and visitors exploring the islands.
